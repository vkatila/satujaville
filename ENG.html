Matarengi Church

Matarengi Church (Swedish: Övertorneå kyrka) is a church in Övertorneå, Norrbotten County, Sweden. The parish’s main church in the town center was built in 1735–1737, and the separate bell tower was completed in 1763. It is one of the best-preserved 18th-century churches in northern Sweden and is home to Sweden’s oldest organs still in use. – Wikipedia –

Lapland View Lodge

Lapland View Lodge is situated on the Luppio Mountain next to Övertorneå. Built in harmony with nature, this landscape hotel was ready to host guests in December 2021 and is now open all year round. In the two main buildings with an adjoining terrace you'll find the reception, restaurant, bar and a cozy lounge with fireplace. In our restaurant we embrace the local cuisine and serve meals with local ingredients from Lapland. The buildings and spa area as well as the 40 hotel cabins have big windows for you to fully enjoy the breathtaking views over the Torne River Valley and the wide area. – Lapland View Lodge –
